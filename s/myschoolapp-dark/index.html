this is a dark theme for myschoolapp.com. when installed, it:

  • restyles all colors to the base16 color scheme, white on black
  • normalizes fonts so that only one font is used across the entire site
  • removes awfulness such as animations, shadow effects, rounded corners, etc.

you can use it by downloading myschoolapp-dark.css above and installing it with Stylish for Firefox or Stylish for Chrome (untested).

here is a summary of progress made so far. full support indicates pages that are completely styled, partial support indicates pages that are in the process of being styled, and no support indicates pages that I have no intention to style/maintain at present.

items marked with an asterisk are expected to change significantly when the school year begins (or at some point during it) in a way that prevents present testing, so their status should be taken with a grain of salt.
